2. Maturity & Compliance Scoring Formulas

Compliance metrics are calculated deterministically to prevent discrepancy during manual auditor validation. The overall domain compliance is computed via a weighted-attestation average model:

Score = Σ (C_i * W_i) / Σ W_i
Where C_i ∈ {0.0, 0.5, 1.0} represents control compliance, and W_i is the assigned criticality weight.

The score mapping factors the following components:

  • Control Response Score (C_i):
    • • Fully Implemented: 1.0 (Attestation true, evidence validated)
    • • Partially Implemented: 0.5 (Attestation true, evidence pending, or policy signed but implementation underway)
    • • Not Implemented: 0.0
  • Evidence Weighting Model: A strict checkbox attestation alone yields a maximum score cap of 50%. The remaining 50% is unlocked only upon uploading cryptographically signed audit evidence (e.g., config outputs, policies) which are hashed and locked on the ledger.

03 / Sovereign AI Architecture & Data Residency

1. LLM Deployment Topology & Zero-Leak Routing

To satisfy national intelligence protection policies, the Navigator includes an active **Sovereign Master Switch (Neural Link)** which triggers an in-browser sandbox model.

  • Standard Active Model: Gemini 1.5 Pro, integrated via enterprise-grade Google Cloud API routes proxied server-side.
  • Sovereign Mode Model: Gemma-4b-GRC-WASM executed directly in the browser's WebAssembly context.
  • Data Residency (Hosting at Rest): Fully resident inside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) hosted at the Google Cloud Dammam Region (me-central2) or Oracle Riyadh Cloud. Under sovereign mode, zero external API outbound packets are sent outside KSA borders.
  • Model Training & Retention Policy: Enterprise API contracts ensure that customer-uploaded policy documents, control responses, and board deliberations are **NEVER** used to train the base model. Retention is strictly locked to transient memory and cleared immediately post-session.

2. SDAIA AI Ethics & Human-in-the-Loop Alignment

The platform's AI engines fully align with SDAIA (Saudi Data & AI Authority) Ethics Principles:

  • Principle of Fairness & Transparency: Boardroom deliberation reasoning, voting models, and risk predictions show full trace parameters, highlighting standard parameters used in the underlying models.
  • Human-in-the-Loop Enforcement: No AI audit recommendation can auto-approve compliance. Every generated draft policy or boardroom proposal requires manual validation and digital signatures from the CISO or Chief Auditor.
  • Hallucination Countermeasures: All policy drafts are structured strictly using local JSON templates from `/data/templates.ts`. If any response exceeds standard syntax restrictions, it is discarded.

3. Risk Scoring Methodology (ISO 31000)

Risk postures use a structured quantitative model based on **Likelihood (1–5) × Impact (1–5)**, visualized on a dynamic interactive 5x5 heatmap. Mitigations automatically suggest associated control mappings from the SAMA CSF and SAMA-NCA index.
